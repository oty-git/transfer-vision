TVS Index

Football Intelligence
Quantified

Unlike traditional statistics, TVS evaluates the cognitive and physical dimensions of play — the qualities that separate good players from great ones.

01
Technique

Passing quality, first touch, weak foot, ball control under defensive pressure.

02
Game Intelligence

Decision-making speed, scanning frequency, positioning awareness, spatial reading.

03
Game Intensity

Performance under pressure, tempo adaptation, competitiveness across match phases.

04
Biomechanics

Acceleration profile, balance mechanics, movement efficiency and energy economy.

05
Potential

Development ceiling, long-term projection, adaptability to higher competitive levels.
